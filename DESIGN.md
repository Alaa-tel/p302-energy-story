## Experience Goals

The experience should feel:

- Educational
- Friendly
- Hopeful
- Sustainable
- Modern

## Visual Style

- Soft green palette
- Off-white backgrounds
- Rounded corners
- Large illustrations
- Card-based layouts
- Large typography
- Smooth animations

## Avoid

- Enterprise dashboard appearance
- Dense tables
- Excessive charts
- Technical energy terminology
- Dark theme

## Desired Feel

A blend of:

- Apple product storytelling
- Google sustainability reports
- Modern climate-focused interactive articles

The experience should feel like a guided story rather than a utility dashboard.
