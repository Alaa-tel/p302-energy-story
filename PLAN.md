# P302 — Interactive Data Story

## Project Title

The Hidden Energy Cost of Your Day

---

## Industry

Energy & Sustainability

---

## Problem

Many people want to reduce their energy consumption and environmental impact, but they often focus on habits that have little effect while overlooking the activities that consume the most energy.

Because energy use is largely invisible, it is difficult to understand which daily behaviors truly matter.

---

## Opportunity

Create an interactive storytelling experience that helps people understand where household energy is actually consumed and which small changes can have the greatest impact.

Rather than presenting raw data, the experience guides users through a narrative that challenges assumptions and reveals surprising insights.

---

## Core Insight

Most people overestimate the impact of turning off lights and unplugging devices, while underestimating the impact of heating, cooling, and hot water usage.

---

## Audience

- Homeowners
- Renters
- Families
- Sustainability-conscious individuals
- People looking to lower utility bills

---

## Story Structure

### Chapter 1
What do people think uses the most energy?

Interactive quiz.

---

### Chapter 2
Where energy actually goes.

Animated energy breakdown.

---

### Chapter 3
Which daily habits matter most?

Interactive comparison of common energy-saving actions.

---

### Chapter 4
Build your personal energy-saving plan.

Users select habits and see their potential annual impact.

---

## Key Interactions

- Quiz selection
- Animated charts
- Toggle controls
- Personalized savings summary
- Scroll-triggered storytelling

---

## Success Criteria

Users leave understanding:

1. Where household energy is actually used.
2. Which habits have the largest impact.
3. How small changes accumulate over time.
4. That sustainable living can be practical and achievable.
